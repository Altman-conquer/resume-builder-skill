# Chinese Resume Writing Guide

Use this guide when rewriting or migrating resume content.

## Structure

For one-page technical resumes, prefer:

1. contact and links;
2. education;
3. internships or work experience;
4. selected projects or research;
5. awards, open source, and skills.

Move the strongest evidence near the top. Remove sections that are only filler.

## Bullet Style

Write bullets as concrete evidence:

- action taken;
- technical method;
- scope or difficulty;
- measurable result.

Prefer specific outcomes over generic praise. Avoid "熟悉", "了解", "认真负责", and repeated technology lists without context.

## Chinese Technical Resume Heuristics

- Keep bullets compact and information-dense.
- Use Arabic numerals for metrics where possible.
- Keep role, organization, and date alignment consistent.
- Avoid exposing unnecessary phone numbers, IDs, addresses, or private repository links.
- Verify all links and make them clickable in final PDF or HTML output.
- For industry applications, target one page unless the user asks for a CV.

## Final Review

Before delivering a resume artifact, check:

- page count;
- overflow and line breaks;
- Chinese font rendering;
- section order;
- broken links;
- duplicate achievements;
- private or sensitive information.
